INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY

We invest where discipline
and conviction converge.

Our portfolio is built on a conviction-based allocation framework — focused on durable advantages, scalable models, and defensible growth. We do not spray capital across opportunities. We concentrate it in companies we understand deeply and support actively.

Conviction-Based Allocation

We invest where structural discipline and market timing converge — focusing on durable advantages, scalable models, and defensible paths to growth.

Governance Before Scale

We establish board-level oversight, KPI accountability, and incentive alignment before growth accelerates — so expansion does not outpace the infrastructure required to sustain it.

Active Partnership

We engage beyond capital — supporting strategic expansion, institutional readiness, and operational execution across every company in the portfolio.

"We are not passive investors. We take equity because we intend to work — to sit alongside founders, challenge assumptions, build systems, and stay in the work until the outcome is real. That is what it means to be an active equity partner."

Marcos Villa — Founder & CEO, Escher Innovations

INVESTMENT THESIS

What we look for in every
company we back.

We go beyond traditional investment. Every portfolio company receives comprehensive strategic support, access to our global network of 150+ countries, and the direct operational involvement of the Escher leadership team.

Criterion 01

Global Market Potential

We prioritize companies with addressable markets extending beyond a single geography — with clear pathways to cross-border expansion and international revenue diversification.

Criterion 02

Scalable Business Model

We invest in companies whose unit economics improve with scale — where growth compounds rather than consumes margins, and where the path to profitability is structural, not speculative.

Criterion 03

Defensible Advantages

We seek companies with proprietary technology, network effects, regulatory moats, or other structural advantages that become harder to replicate as the business grows and compounds.

Criterion 04

Exceptional Founding Teams

We back people first. We look for founders with domain expertise, intellectual honesty, the resilience to navigate adversity, and the character to make the right decisions under pressure.
