Marcos Villa began researching and trading stocks, ETFs, and currencies at age 15 in Cherry Hill, New Jersey. What started as intellectual curiosity became a professional foundation built on technical analysis, capital markets, and macroeconomic strategy.

By his early twenties he was advising ultra-high-net-worth individuals, government officials, and multinational corporations — recognized as among the top 0.0001% of financial practitioners globally. He founded Escher Innovations on a single belief: that the barriers separating great ideas from great outcomes are systemic — not personal — and therefore fixable. PROPRIETARY FRAMEWORK

The Escher Persona Model™ —
leadership as a learnable skill.

Developed from decades of behavioral psychology and cognitive science, the EPM is our proprietary system for identifying, cultivating, and sustaining the mindsets and behaviors that drive strategic leadership at every level of an organization. Used in 150+ countries with startups, Fortune 500s, and governments.

Component 01 — Mindsets

Cognitive & Emotional Anchors

The beliefs and orientations that shape how an individual interprets challenges, leads others, and grows over time.

  • Strategic self-awareness
  • Growth orientation
  • Adaptive confidence
  • Purpose-driven intention
Component 02 — Behaviors

Thriving & Inspiring Actions

Tactical behaviors that translate mindset into movement — classified into personal thriving and leadership impact.

  • Thriving: rest, focus, emotional regulation
  • Thriving: strategic planning, resource prioritization
  • Inspiring: inclusion, coaching others
  • Inspiring: decisive action, communication clarity
Component 03 — Outcomes

Trackable, Measurable Results

The beneficial results that emerge from aligned mindsets and consistent behaviors — tracked and reported continuously.

  • Resilience under pressure
  • Team performance & organizational alignment
  • Innovation output
  • Job satisfaction & talent retention
