About Escher  ·  What We Do

We don't advise.
We invest, build, and grow.

Escher Innovations partners directly with founders, organizations, and governments — bringing capital, institutional-grade strategy, and hands-on execution to close the gap between ambition and outcome.

Strategic Advisory Capital Deployment Digital Transformation Operations and Execution Sales and Growth
THE ESCHER DIFFERENCE

Three commitments that define
every engagement we take.

Most advisory firms sell their time and move on. We take equity positions, work alongside you through implementation, and measure our success by the durability of what we build together. This is not a service offering — it is a partnership model built around aligned incentives and shared outcomes.

"We only win when you do. That alignment is not a tagline — it is the structural reason our advice is different from every other firm in the market. When we take equity, every recommendation is made with the same urgency and precision we would apply to our own organization."

Marcos Villa — Founder & CEO

Commitment 01

We Take Ownership

We take equity positions in the companies we believe in. Our incentives are permanently tied to your outcome — no retainer, no disengagement after delivery. We are in it for the long run because we have structured it that way from day one.

Commitment 02

Structure Before Speed

Scaling without the right foundation is how businesses lose control. We put governance, accountability, and financial structure in place first — so when capital comes in, your business is built to handle it without fracturing under the pressure of its own growth.

Commitment 03

More Than a Check

Capital alone does not build great companies. We work alongside you on strategy, operations, and execution — the way a seasoned co-founder would, backed by decades of cross-sector experience and a global network operating across 150+ countries.

FIVE SERVICE AREAS

End-to-end capability.
One integrated partnership.

Every engagement draws from five integrated practice areas. We apply what the situation requires — not what fits a standard retainer model.

01

Strategic Planning and Development

Comprehensive strategies built through rigorous market research, competitive analysis, and scenario planning — aligned to your vision, your market position, and the structural realities of your industry. We do not produce generic frameworks. We build precise plans for specific organizations in specific conditions.

What we deliver

  • Capital deployment strategy for UHNWIs and institutions
  • Market trend analysis and long-term forecasting
  • International and domestic market expansion planning
  • Competitive positioning and differentiation strategy

Who it is for

  • Founders preparing for institutional fundraising
  • Organizations entering new geographic markets
  • Companies repositioning in response to market shifts
  • Investors seeking structured capital deployment frameworks
02

Financial Management and Advisory

Expert guidance to optimize financial performance, restructure capital, and execute high-stakes transactions with precision and institutional-grade discipline. We position businesses correctly before approaching capital markets — because how you arrive determines what terms you receive.

What we deliver

  • Capital raising and debt restructuring advisory
  • Mergers, acquisitions, and investment analysis
  • Budget planning and long-term financial modeling
  • Business credit positioning and lender preparation

Who it is for

  • Companies preparing for their first institutional raise
  • Organizations with complex capital structure challenges
  • Founders navigating M&A processes for the first time
  • Businesses that have been declined and do not know why
03

Digital Transformation and Technology

We help organizations modernize operations, implement AI and data intelligence systems, and build the digital infrastructure that creates durable competitive advantage. Technology without strategy produces technical debt. We build both simultaneously — the vision and the systems to execute it.

What we deliver

  • Augmented reality solutions for enterprise and government
  • AI implementation and data analytics architecture
  • Cloud migration and workflow automation
  • CRM infrastructure and operational system design

Who it is for

  • Organizations with fragmented or outdated technology stacks
  • Companies implementing AI without a strategic framework
  • Government agencies modernizing service delivery
  • Businesses building operational infrastructure for scale
04

Operations, Logistics, and Execution

We identify structural inefficiencies and implement tailored solutions that drive productivity, reduce operational cost, and align every function of the business toward its strategic objectives. Most organizations do not fail from bad strategy — they fail from poor execution infrastructure.

What we deliver

  • Process optimization and workflow redesign
  • Supply chain management and resilience planning
  • Organizational design and governance structure
  • Performance management systems and KPI frameworks

Who it is for

  • Scaling companies outgrowing their current systems
  • Organizations with high operational cost and low output
  • Businesses preparing for rapid headcount or revenue growth
  • Companies building for institutional-grade operational maturity
05

Sales, Marketing, and Brand Development

From go-to-market strategy to full brand identity, we build the commercial infrastructure that generates revenue, captures market share, and creates lasting brand equity. A strong product without strong commercial systems stays invisible. We ensure what you build gets to the people who need it.

What we deliver

  • Sales strategy and go-to-market execution
  • Brand identity, messaging, and visual positioning
  • Lead generation and digital marketing infrastructure
  • Customer acquisition and revenue system design

Who it is for

  • Founders who have built a product but cannot scale revenue
  • Companies entering new markets without an established brand
  • Organizations rebuilding commercial strategy after stagnation
  • Businesses seeking institutional-grade market positioning
HOW WE WORK

Four stages. One
continuous engagement.

We do not deliver a report and leave. Every engagement is structured around four stages that run continuously — so strategy, execution, measurement, and improvement are always operating in parallel.

01
Discovery

Understand Before We Act

A rigorous diagnostic of your current state, strategic objectives, and the specific gaps between them. We do not prescribe before we understand — and we understand at depth before we recommend a single action.

02
Architecture

Design the System

We translate diagnosis into a structured transformation plan covering strategy, capital structure, governance, technology, and operations — built simultaneously so the infrastructure exists to carry the strategy forward.

03
Execution

Work Alongside You

We engage beyond advisory. Our team works alongside yours — managing implementation, resolving blockers in real time, and making adjustments as market conditions evolve. Strategy lives or dies in execution.

04
Measurement

Track and Compound

We establish clear KPIs, accountability structures, and insight loops from day one. Progress is visible and reviewed continuously — so the organization builds the capacity to grow stronger over time, independent of us.
